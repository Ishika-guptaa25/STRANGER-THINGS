THE UPSIDE DOWN

A dark parallel dimension existing alongside the human world. It is a place of decay and death, a plane out of phase, a place of monsters. It is right next to you and you don't even see it.

The Upside Down is cold and dark, an echo of our world covered in vines and spores. Time moves differently there, and the Mind Flayer watches from the shadows, waiting for the moment to strike.

THE STORY

SEASON 1

When Will Byers disappears in the small town of Hawkins, his friends embark on a dangerous quest to find him. They discover Eleven, a mysterious girl with supernatural powers, and uncover a secret government experiment that has torn open a gateway to another dimension.

SEASON 2

A year after Will's return, the horror deepens. The Mind Flayer, a powerful entity from the Upside Down, begins to possess Will and spread its influence across Hawkins. The party must unite to close the gate before the darkness consumes everything.

SEASON 3

Summer brings new dangers as a secret Soviet facility beneath the Starcourt Mall attempts to reopen the gate. The Mind Flayer returns with a vengeance, building an army from the possessed residents of Hawkins in a terrifying display of power.

SEASON 4

Six months after the Battle of Starcourt, a new and horrifying supernatural threat surfaces in Hawkins. As Eleven struggles without her powers, the friends are separated and must fight their greatest enemy yet: Vecna, the mastermind behind it all.
